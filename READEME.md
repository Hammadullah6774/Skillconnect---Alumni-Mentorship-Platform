### 1. Project Theme
**SkillConnect** is an Alumni Mentorship Platform designed to connect students with alumni for mentorship, guidance, and professional networking. It allows users to book mentorship sessions, chat with mentors, update profiles, and provide feedback.

### 3. Project Overview
SkillConnect enables students to:
- Log in as alumni or students.
- Connect and schedule sessions with alumni mentors.
- Update their personal profiles.
- Communicate with mentors through chat.
- Provide feedback for continuous improvement.
